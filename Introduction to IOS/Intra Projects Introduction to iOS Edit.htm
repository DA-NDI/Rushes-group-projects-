    Introduction
    

    
This schedule contains areas where you MUST decide, based on your own experience, how to control a requested feature. 

    

    We ask you, for the good progress of this notation: 
 
    

    - To remain courteous, polite, respectful, constructive, during this exchange. The bond of trust between the community 42 and you depends on it. 

    - To highlight with the person noted (or the group) the possible malfunctions. 

    - To accept that there may sometimes be differences of interpretation on the subject's requests or the extent of the functionalities. Stay open-minded about the other's vision (is it right or wrong?), And write as honestly as you can. 

    

    If there is cheating, the notation stops. For the record, a case of cheating corresponds to a code which is not made or not reproducible by its author, or the use of elements explicitly prohibited or which aim to distort the educational role of the project. To use with calm, precaution, and discernment.

    

    Good defense to all!

    

    

    

    Guidelines
    

    
If the repository contains more files than requested in the subject, this is by no means a problem.

    

    In the case of a false exercise, the correction continues until the end: the exercises are optional and not compulsory.

    

    The application must be adapted to the iPhone 6.

    preliminaries
    

    

    

    Question 1 
    

    
Does the application compile without errors in the simulator and without crashing? 

    

    Otherwise, stop the correction.

    

    

    

    

    





    

    

    

    

    

    

    features
    

    

    

    Question 2 
    

    
The loading view is different than the default one. There is at least one image and one text that is not just the name of the application.

    If the elements do not display properly or exit the screen do not put the points.

    

    

    

    

    





    

    

    Question 3 
    

    
A new view must be present in addition to the two basic, so there must be at least 3 accessible from the bottom tabbar.

    There must be at least one custom icon for one of the views, and again the right size without overflowing and good quality.

    

    

    

    

    





    

    

    Question 4 
    

    
On one of the views there must be a list that includes several elements we must be able to add in the code and they must appear clearly.

    

    

    

    

    

    





    

    

    Question 5 
    

    
Check that there is a map in which you can move and zoom in / out.

    

    

    

    

    





    

    

    Question 6 
    

    
The map is in hybrid or satellite mode at the opening of the view. -> 1 points
 
    

    There is a visible pin which is placed at the location of 42 at the opening of the view. -> 2 points
 
    

    The scale is zoomed enough on the map (at your discretion). -> 2 points

    

    

    

    

    Rate it from 0 (failed) through 5 (excellent)
    

    

    

    

    Question 7 
    

    
There is a FUNCTIONAL button for each mode of the map (standard, satellite, hybrid).

    

    

    

    

    





    

    

    Question 8 
    

    
There is a geolocation button that centers on the map. -> 3 points
 
    

    The zoom is placed at a reasonable height (at your discretion) -> 2 points
 
    

    Test well to change the location in the simulator to verify that it works for real. Otherwise -> cheat

    

    

    

    

    Rate it from 0 (failed) through 5 (excellent)
    

    

    

    

    Question 9 
    

    
All the places entered in your code are present in the list -> 1 point
 
    

    A click on one of the places returns a detailed view of the map with a pin at the locality and the information of the pin (title, sub- title ...) -> 4 points
 
    

    If when you change the places in the code (for example, their coordinates) and that does not change in the list -> cheat then
 
    check that the information of places entered a only once in the code, not once for the map and once for the list.

    

    

    

    

    Rate it from 0 (failed) through 5 (excellent)

    • Custom icons for your buttons.

    • Have added somewhere in your app the answer to the riddle.

    

    Note the bonuses included according to their difficulty, their originality, all at the discretion of the corrector.
